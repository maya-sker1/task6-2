    Economical

    Here at puffin we specialise in providing economical and eco-friendly alternatives to polystyrene boxes.

    Eco-Friendly

    Puffin Packaging is made from sheep’s wool, a natural insulator that is far more effective at keeping produce at the correct temperature than other insulation.

    So much so that in tests our pouches kept food safely chilled for a full 24 hours* - 60% longer than other alternatives.

    Why Choose Puffin?

    Not to blow our own trumpet, but there are lots of reasons to make the change to Puffin today. From our commitment to the environment, to our outstanding track record in temperature insulated packaging, to the fact that it just looks so good. Your packaging is our priority.

    Made With 100% Sheep's Wool

    Our liners and pouches are filled with biodegradable sheep's wool - natural insulation at its finest.

    100% Compostable & Biodegradable

    The insulation in our liners and pouches is 100% biodegradable and 100% compostable.

    A Cost Effective Solution

    We're the only company in the UK providing eco-friendly packaging at affordable prices. 

    Highly Efficient & Effective

    Your produce will be kept fresh at the right temperature for over 24 hours.

    Planet Friendly & Responsible

    Our materials are responsibly sourced and are recyclable or reusable.
